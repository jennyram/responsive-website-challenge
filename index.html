Antonine Gate HIIT Club

Work Hard. Sweat A Lot. Drink Wine.

Welcome friend.

Join our high intensity interval training workout sessions. We meet three times a week to train hard, complain a lot, then drink our wine sort of guilt-free.

We can't promise results, but we can promise burpees, mountain climbers, other awful exercises, but a lot of fun on the side.

"There is nothing pleasurable about HIIT. It's awful. But the Antonine Gate HIIT Club makes it bearable, and the company and post-workout drinks make it the highlight of my week."

Jenny R, HIIT attendee.

We meet every Monday, Wednesday and Friday at 7.30pm, with wine to follow.
